BN Kre8tive is a community-driven creative economy initiative training, equipping and connecting Benue State's designers, filmmakers, musicians, artists and builders, proving that craft is a real career, not a backup plan.

Join the Community Partner With Us
Scroll
For too long, Benue's young people have been told politics is the only route to a good life. We're building proof of a second path.

Benue is full of gifted young people in graphic design, technology, fine art, music and film, but most have nowhere to learn, no one to mentor them, no equipment to start with, and no clear sense that creativity can be a viable career.

The global creative economy generates hundreds of billions of dollars a year. BN Kre8tive exists to make that opportunity visible, reachable and real for a young person in Makurdi, Gboko, Otukpo or Katsina-Ala.

Core initiatives

A portfolio of recurring programmes, not one-off events

Skill-building

The Academy

Hands-on training from 1 day to 4 weeks, led by working professionals, beginner to monetize-ready, in-person and recorded.

Guidance

Mentorship Circles

3–6 month pairings with established creatives, plus monthly check-ins and live "Ask an Expert" sessions.

Access

The Hub

A shared Makurdi space with cameras, mics, computers and software, the single biggest barrier, solved together.

Reach

Campus Outreach

Career days, taster workshops and a campus ambassador at every priority institution across the state.

Visibility

Showcases & Festival

Quarterly showcase nights and an annual flagship festival that puts members in front of buyers and press.

Community

Digital Home

Discipline-based groups, a content calendar and a shared library of assets, presets and opportunities.
